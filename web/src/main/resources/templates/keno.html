How to play

  1. Pick numbers. Tap up to 10 cells on the board (1–80). You can also use Quick Pick to auto-fill the rest at random.
  2. Set a stake between 10 and 500 credits, then Deal.
  3. 20 numbers are drawn from the same 1–80 pool. They light up on the board one-by-one. Cells that match your picks and get drawn count as hits.
  4. The payout depends on how many spots you picked and how many of those got hit — see the table below. Bigger spot counts unlock bigger top-end multipliers but need more hits to start paying (e.g. a 10-spot ticket needs 6+ hits to pay).
  5. On a winning round your stake is multiplied; on a losing round you lose the stake. A small slice of every loss feeds the per-server Jackpot Pool at the top of the page — any future winning round on this server has a chance to claim the entire pool on top of its payout.
