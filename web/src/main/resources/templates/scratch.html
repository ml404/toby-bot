🎟️ Scratch

Buy a 9-cell scratchcard. Match 5+ of any symbol to win; payouts scale with match count. Bet 10-500 credits.

Balance: 0 credits

Rules

  • Buy a ticket for any stake between 10 and 500 credits.
  • The 3×3 card has 9 cells, each a random symbol from a weighted pool (4🍒, 3🍋, 2🔔, 1⭐).
  • Click each cell to scratch it off, OR hit "Reveal all".
  • Match 5+ of one symbol → win the symbol's payout × (match count − 4).
  • Base payouts: 🍒=1×, 🍋=2×, 🔔=8×, ⭐=40×. So 5⭐=40×, 9⭐=200× stake.
