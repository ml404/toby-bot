🂡 Solo blackjack

Bet between 10 and 500 credits per hand. Dealer stands on all 17. Natural blackjack pays 3:2.

Place your bet

Wallet: 0 credits

Dealer

You

Rules

  • Beat the dealer's hand without going over 21. Face cards count as 10; aces count as 1 or 11.
  • Hit draws another card; Stand keeps your total. You bust at 22+ and lose your stake.
  • Dealer hits until 17 or higher (stands on soft 17 by default).
  • A two-card 21 (a "natural blackjack") pays 3:2. Any other win pays 1:1. Pushes refund your stake.
  • Double down on your first two cards: doubles your stake, you draw exactly one more card.
  • Split a matching pair (e.g. 8-8) into two hands — each plays its own ante. Up to 4 split hands; split aces auto-stand on one card.
