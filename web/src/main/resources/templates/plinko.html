🟢 Plinko

Drop a ball through 8 peg rows; it lands in one of 9 buckets. Pick a risk profile — higher risk swings the outer multipliers up and adds bust buckets in the middle. Bet 10 to 500 credits.

Balance: 0 credits

Payout tables (× stake)

Risk 0

All three profiles target ~0.90 RTP. Outer buckets pay big; center buckets refund (×1), partially lose (×0.4–0.7), or bust (×0).

Rules

  • Pick a risk profile (LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH) and a stake.
  • Each of the 8 rows flips a fair coin to nudge the ball left or right.
  • The ball settles into one of 9 buckets; that bucket's multiplier is your payout.
  • Multiplier > 1 = win, = 1 = refund, < 1 = partial or full loss.
  • Wins roll for the jackpot pool; losses tribute the lost portion of the stake.
