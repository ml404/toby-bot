Daily streak & achievements

Daily-claim rewards scale by streak length: min(base + per_day_bonus × (streak − 1), max). Both XP and credit grants bypass their respective daily caps. Set per-day bonus to 0 to make rewards flat. The achievement-announce channel posts a public shoutout in addition to the unlocker's DM — leave it blank to keep unlocks DM-only.

Title gating

Set a minimum level a user must reach before they can buy a title. Crossing that level also auto-unlocks the title for free, so this doubles as a level-up reward path. 0 means "no gate".
