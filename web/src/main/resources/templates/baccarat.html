Rules

  • Card values: A=1, 2–9 face value, 10/J/Q/K=0. Hand total is the sum mod 10 (so 7+8=5).
  • Each side starts with two cards. If either has a total of 8 or 9, that's a Natural — both hands stand.
  • Otherwise: Player draws a third card on totals 0–5, stands on 6–7. Banker's draw rule depends on whether Player drew and the value of Player's third card (the standard Punto Banco tableau).
  • Highest total after at most one extra card per side wins.
  • Winning bets: Player pays 2.00×, Banker pays 1.95× (5% commission on the win), Tie pays 9.00×.
  • On a tied game, Player and Banker side bets are refunded.
