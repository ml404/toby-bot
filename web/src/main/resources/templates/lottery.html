How payouts work

The day's prize pool splits across match tiers. 0 or 1 matches pay nothing — those tickets fund tomorrow's prizes (and feed the jackpot via the revenue split). Empty tiers and rounding remainders roll back into the per-server jackpot pool.

Matches Prize pool share Notes
5 / 5 60% Top tier — split equally if multiple jackpot winners. Second tier. Third tier — frequent at this odds level. Consolation tier — small return.
0–1 / 5 0% Lose stake — funds tomorrow's pool.

How payouts work

Three winners are picked at close-time, weighted by how many tickets each user holds. More tickets = higher chance of being drawn (and of being drawn into a higher tier). Empty tiers and rounding remainders roll back into the per-server jackpot pool.

Tier Prize pool share Notes
1st50% Top tier — drawn first, weighted by ticket count.
2nd30% Second tier — remaining holders re-weighted after 1st is removed.
3rd20% Third tier — same.
