What's a "cube"? A custom pool of Magic cards you draft from instead of sealed boosters.

Two ways to define one:

  • Scryfall search — describe a set of cards, e.g. set:vow, t:dragon, cube:vintage.
  • My card list — paste your own decklist, one card per line (an optional count works too, like 10 Forest).

What's "as-fan"? The average number of a given kind of card you open per pack — so you can see your draft isn't flooded with lands or starved of removal.
